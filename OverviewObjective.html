Overview & Objective



OVERVIEW

The primary responsibility of an information systems consultant is to offer specialized counsel and recommendations to organizations pertaining to their information systems, technological infrastructure, and data handling. Information systems consultants have a pivotal role in aiding companies in enhancing their technology and data-related procedures to attain their intended objectives. This project delves into the significance of information systems within the business context and the advantages that can result from their effective implementation.



OBJECTIVE OF THIS PROJECT

The objective of this project is to provide students with practical insights into the role of Information Systems in business while highlighting the overlap between software development and computer science. It aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications, equipping students with essential skills to solve practical business challenges, make data-driven decisions, and understand the intersection of Information Systems with software development and computer science.
